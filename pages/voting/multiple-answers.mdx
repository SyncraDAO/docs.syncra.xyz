# Approval with Multiple Answers

import {Callout} from 'nextra-theme-docs'

<Callout type="info">
  **Availability**
  - Wallet-based strategy
</Callout>

Approval with Multiple Answers is a flexible voting strategy available in wallet-based DAO. Participants can vote for multiple answers, and all their votes are counted equally. This approach is useful in situations where participants can support multiple options simultaneously, providing a more inclusive decision-making process.
