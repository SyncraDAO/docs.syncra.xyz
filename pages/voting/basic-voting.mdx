# Basic Voting or Referendum

import {Callout} from 'nextra-theme-docs'

<Callout type="info">
  **Availability**
  - Wallet-based strategy
  - Token-based strategy
</Callout>

Basic Voting, also known as a Referendum, is a simple and widely used voting strategy. 

Participants cast their votes as:
- YES
- NO
- ABSTAIN

Each vote carries equal weight, making it a straightforward way to gauge community sentiment and make decisions.
